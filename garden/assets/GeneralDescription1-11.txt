GuL (Flower)Music and text: Atai OgonbaevPerformer: Zainidin lmanalievAtai Ogombaev was a well-known singer and komuz player from Talas, in western Kyrgyzstan. In this song from the early 1930s, he expresses the optimism of those times. The nightingale is a central image of Sufi-inspired poetry, and its spiritual symbolism resonates in Atai's verse.
