KUIDUM CHOK (I Burn, I Smoulder like Charcoal)Composero Atai Ogonbaev (1900-1949)Text: Atai OgonbaevPerformer: Zainidin lmanaliev, vocal, komuzIn this autobiographical song, Atai Ogonbaev, a famous Kyrgyz bard, recounts his youthful romance with the daughter of a wealthy neighbor. To prevent the girl from marrying a poor musician, her family secretly gave her in marriage to a boy from a distant settlement. Atai composed this song after a fruitless two-year search for his beloved.
